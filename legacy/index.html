How It Works

Three steps.
Zero manual work.

Zentra plugs into your existing lead sources and handles the entire follow-up cycle — call, qualify, book — without involving your team.

01 — Capture
Lead submits a form
A new lead fills in your Facebook ad, website, or landing page. Zentra receives it instantly via webhook or native integration.
02 — Call
AI calls in under 60 seconds
Zentra's AI voice agent calls the lead immediately. It speaks naturally in English or Bahasa Malaysia, qualifies them, and handles objections.
03 — Convert
Appointment is booked
Qualified leads are booked into your calendar automatically. Your team gets a notification and shows up to close — not to chase.
